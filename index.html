About The Workshop

The CV4Clinic 2026 workshop aims to bridge advanced computer vision techniques and the complex realities of clinical practice. While CVPR continues to drive progress in imaging, perception, and reasoning, this workshop explores how such advances can translate into real-world medical impacts. By convening experts from academia, industry, and healthcare, CV4Clinic 2026 will focus on key issues of model effectiveness, reliability, and reproducibility in clinical deployment; critically examine the opportunities and challenges of emerging paradigms such as foundation and generative models; and explore future directions for socially meaningful and clinically grounded medical AI.

Call for Papers

We welcome submissions of long papers (4-8 pages, excluding references) for oral or poster presentation at the workshop. Accepted papers will be published in the CVPR 2026 Workshop Proceedings.
